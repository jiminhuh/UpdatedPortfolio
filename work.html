Have you ever wanted to know if the new restaurant around the block was worth it, but all you see are sponsored or fake posts promoting the same thing? With OTPYG, you can search for your favorite location or restaurant and get the what the real people think... in tweets. Using the Google & Twitter API, you will receive real-time tweets associated with your search result, giving you the most real and up to date thoughts people have about it. You want to know if Franklin's BBQ has a wait? Give OTPYG a try (It probably does have a wait).
